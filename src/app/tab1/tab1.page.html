About UrbanEstate

UrbanEstate is a modern real estate app designed for the Irish market. It connects buyers and renters with trusted local agents, making it easy to compare homes, view details, and schedule a visit in minutes.

This version of the app is a learning project built with Ionic, Angular, Firebase Authentication and responsive design – showing how a real property platform could look and behave on mobile devices.
